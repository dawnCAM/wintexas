Important Legal Notice
Texas Election Code § 18.067
"A person commits an offense if the person uses information in connection with advertising or promoting commercial products or services that the person knows was obtained under Section 18.066. An offense under this section is a Class A misdemeanor."
What this means in plain English:
✓
Using voter information to contact people for civic, political, or GOTV purposes is allowed
✗
Using voter information to advertise or sell commercial products or services is a Class A misdemeanor
✗
Sharing or selling voter data with businesses or third parties is not allowed
